Corona Apocalypse

This is a Virtual Reality shooter game in first-person using the Oculus Quest 2. The theme of the game is the Coronavirus. This game is set in a zombie apocalypse type of scene, with derelict houses, and people walking around who have been infected by the virus. In this game the player has a mask shooter in which they must shoot masks at the infected people and also avoid making contact with them by keeping socially distant.

AR Haunted House Poster

This is an Augmented Reality poster using Vuforia and Unity. The poster that I created was a haunted house. To use it, you must scan the QR code on the bottom right-hand corner, download the application, then run it and view the poster through your phone to see the ghost.
